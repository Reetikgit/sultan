Mohammad Arshad Shamim

CEO
Juggling multiple positions of responsibilities,Mohammad Arshad Shamim is an Indian entrepreneur, who has not only founded Sultan Apparels Pvt Ltd but is also the director of the company. Established on 27th January,1996. Sultan moved from selling just Kurtas and sherwanis to everything ethnic, especially wedding wear.
Providing a diverse range of sherwanis, Kurtas, ethnic apparels and kid's wear, . Aiming to deliver absolute satisfaction, Sultan apparels consists of a variety of collections that will most certainly satiate customers from all over the country to across the border.
